A mission plan is an executable model used to command an autonomous resource.


The plan includes a collection of waypoints, a series of activities, and a resource assignment.
Mission plans can be parameterized and saved as templates for future missions.
Maps can be saved separately for reuse.
Activities can be saved separately for reuse.
Reources are defined in terms of their hardware and capabilities. For example,
a small boat might have a single drive motor plus a rudder, or it might have two
drives and steer by thrust differentials. Lower level activities, such as steering
and battery monitoring, are associated with the resource.
